Product Description

The Summit Series 8×16 is built for gardeners who are serious about extending their growing season. With 128 square feet of interior space and a 9-foot peak height, you can grow everything from tomatoes to tropical plants year-round in most climates.

Twin-wall 6mm polycarbonate panels provide 80% light transmission while blocking 99% of UV — better insulation than glass with superior impact resistance. The heavy-duty aluminum frame is pre-drilled and labeled for straightforward assembly.

  • 6mm twin-wall polycarbonate panels, 10-year warranty
  • Heavy-duty anodized aluminum frame
  • Two roof vents + sliding door included
  • Wind rated to 60mph, snow load 15 lbs/sq ft
  • Assembly time: approx. 6–8 hours, 2 people recommended

Specifications

Dimensions: 8ft wide × 16ft long × 9ft peak height
