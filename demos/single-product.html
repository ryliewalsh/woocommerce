Description

The Automatic Roof Vent Opener uses a temperature-sensitive wax cylinder to automatically open your greenhouse roof vent when temperatures rise, and close it as temperatures drop — no electricity, no wiring, no manual intervention required.

Compatible with all Grandio greenhouse models and most standard roof vents up to 18 inches wide. The wax-filled cylinder expands as it heats and contracts as it cools, providing reliable operation season after season.

  • No electricity required — fully mechanical operation
  • Opens automatically at your chosen temperature threshold
  • Adjustable opening temperature via set screw
  • Compatible with vents up to 18" wide
  • Rated for 5+ years of daily operation
  • Includes all mounting hardware
